Troubleshooting

Seeing a "Cannot verify login" error? Read on...

To fix in Chrome you can follow these steps. Other browsers will allow for similar settings tweaks to be made.

  1. Open Chrome's settings page by selecting the "Chrome" menu and then "Settings"
  2. On the settings page, click "Privacy and security" in the left sidebar (you may need to open the sidebar first on devices with smaller screens)
  3. On the privacy settings page window, click "Third-party cookies"
  4. On the cookie settings page, scroll to the bottom and add [*.]google.com and [*.]gutools.co.uk as sites which are allowed to use third party cookies
  5. Go back to Workflow and refresh the page/tab
