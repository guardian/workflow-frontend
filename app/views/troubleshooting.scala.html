Troubleshooting

Seeing a "Cannot verify login" error? Read on...

To fix in...

Chrome

  1. Open Preferences by selecting the "Chrome" menu and then "Preferences"
  2. Scroll down and click on "Show Advanced settings..."
  3. Click "Content settings..." under Privacy
  4. In the new "Content settings" window, click "Manage exceptions..."
  5. In the new "Cookie and site data exceptions" window, paste [*.]google.com into a blank box
  6. Click Done then Done
  7. Go back to Workflow and refresh the page/tab

Need more help? Please contact the Workflow team.
