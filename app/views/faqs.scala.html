FAQs

  • What happens if I delete an item from workflow?

    Items that are deleted from workflow will disappear from the UI, and be permanently deleted (from workflow, not from composer) after 24 hours.

    If you have accidentally removed an item and require it back, you can contact the Workflow team

  • How long are items shown?

    Items are hidden from the interface once they fulfill all of the following criteria:

    • Published
    • In 'Final' state
    • Has not been modified in the last 24 hours

    Items can be brought back into view by changing their status in Composer.

    Workflow will not hide articles that have not been published.
