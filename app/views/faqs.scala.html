FAQS

    What happens if I delete an item from workflow?

    Items that are deleted from workflow will go to an archive table. If you have accidently removed an item and require it back, you can contact the Workflow team and a developer will be able to restore this.

    How long are items shown?

    Items are hidden from the interface once they fulfill all of the following criteria:

    • Published
    • In 'Final' state
    • Last modified for longer than a day

    Items can be brought back into view by changing their status on Composer

    Workflow will not hide articles that have not been published
